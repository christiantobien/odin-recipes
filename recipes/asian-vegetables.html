The Best Pan Of Riz and Vegetables

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Carrots
  • Paprika
  • Onions
  • Coconut Milk
  • Chicken
  • Red Curry

Steps To Do

  1. Cut onions, paprika, carrots in space pieces
  2. Heat it up in the pan. Insert chicken afterwards
  3. When everything is cooked, insert red curry and coconut mil
  4. Boil portion of rice
  5. Add boiled rice to pan and mix it up
