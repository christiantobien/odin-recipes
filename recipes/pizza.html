The Famous Polish Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Meat
  • Red Onions
  • Bacon
  • Speck
  • Mozzarella
  • Cheese

Steps To Do

  1. Prepare ingredients and cut onions and tomatoes into pieces
  2. Take the Dough and start with the tomato sauce putting on it
  3. Now the right order matters:
    1. Put half of the cheese on it
    2. Add cherry tomatoes
    3. Add meat and speck
    4. Add speck and onion
    5. Add mozzarella and the second part of the cheese
  4. Bake the pizza for ~20 Minutes at 200 Degrees Celsius
