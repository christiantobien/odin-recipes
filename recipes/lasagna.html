The Best Lasagna Recipe Of The World

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Plats
  • Pieces of Tomato
  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps To Do

  1. Cook onion, meat, tomatoes together. Use a pan for that
  2. For the bechamel: Warm the butter and insert flour and milch until you have a white cream
  3. Now it's time for the baking:
    1. One line of Noodles
    2. One line of tomato-meat-mix
    3. One line of bechamel sauce
  4. Repeat those steps until the baking is filled (usually 2-3 times)
  5. Put the cheese on top of it
  6. Bake the lasagna for ~40 Minutes at 200 Degrees Celsius
